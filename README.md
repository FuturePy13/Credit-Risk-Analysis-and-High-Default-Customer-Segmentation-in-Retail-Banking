# Credit-Risk-Analysis-and-High-Default-Customer-Segmentation-in-Retail-Banking

This table store every customer with a loan process in the bank. Every row represent a single customer with diferent features tracked by the company.
### Business Variables

* **customer_id**: Unique identifier for each customer **(Used for tracking)**

* **age**: Age of the customer **(Can influence financial stability and risk behavior)**

* **monthly_income**: Customer monthly income in USD **(Key variable to assess repayment capacity)**

* **credit_score**: Customer's Creditworhtiness **(Lower scores indicates higher risk)**

* **loan_amount**: Total amount of the loan granted **(Higher amounts increase exposure and potential loss)**

* **loan_term_months**: Duration of the loan in months **(Long terms may increase uncertainly and risk)**

* **interest_rate**: Annual interest rate applied to the loan **(Reflects how the bank prices risk)**

* **default**: Binary variable indicating wether the customer default (1) or not (0)

* **loan_status**: Categorical version of loan outcome
